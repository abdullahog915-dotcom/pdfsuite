Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

At PDF SUITE, we take your privacy seriously. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.

1. File Security

We understand that your documents are important. Here is how we handle them:

  • Automatic Deletion: All files uploaded to our servers are processed immediately and are automatically deleted after 1 hour or after the download is complete.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not store your files on our database or permanent storage.
  • Encryption: All data transfer is secured using SSL/TLS encryption.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data (like names, emails, or phone numbers) unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., contacting support).

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies only to ensure the website functions correctly. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer: Abdullah Khan.
